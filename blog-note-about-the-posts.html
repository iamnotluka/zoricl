Note about the posts

2026-04-25 · meta · writing

My posts are notes to myself. Writing is a mechanism I use to measure my own growth.

Publishing a thought locks it in time so that later, when I've learned more, the disagreement with my old self becomes evident. This disagreement is not so much a problem as it is evidence of my growth.

Each post is a benchmark I unconsciously set for my future self to surpass. This also consequently proves to me that what I believe right now is also a snapshot, temporary, and probably wrong somewhere.

2026 Luka Zoric Brisbane, AU
