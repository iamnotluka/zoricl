##### Business and Marketing
* [The Almanak of Naval Ravikant](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Almanak+of+Naval+Ravikant+buy)
  * Insights into the entrepreneurship of a successful investor with practical advice on money, time, productivity, and happiness.
* [Building a StoryBrand: Clarify Your Message So Customers Will Listen - Donald Miller](https://www.google.com/search?q=Building+a+StoryBrand+Donald+Miller+buy)
  * Understand how to craft a brand message that genuinely connects with customers.
* [Scientific Advertising - Claude C. Hopkins](https://www.google.com/search?q=Scientific+Advertising+Claude+C.+Hopkins+buy)
  * OG book on advertising from early 1900s.
* [High Output Management - Andrew S. Grove](https://www.google.com/search?q=High+Output+Management+Andrew+S.+Grove+buy)
  * Learn about optimizing team productivity and management processes.
* [The One Minute Sales Person - Spencer Johnson](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+One+Minute+Sales+Person+Spencer+Johnson+buy)
  * Quick strategies for effective sales. Quick read and fairly practical.
* [The Adweek Copywriting Handbook: The Ultimate Guide to Writing Powerful Advertising and Marketing Copy from One of America's Top Copywriters](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Adweek+Copywriting+Handbook+buy)
  * A comprehensive guide to crafting compelling advertising and marketing copy from a top industry expert.

##### Economics and Finance
* [The Psychology of Money - Morgan Housel](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Psychology+of+Money+Morgan+Housel+buy)
  * Deeper emotional and psychological aspects of money.
* [The Changing World Order - Ray Dalio](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Changing+World+Order+Ray+Dalio+buy)
  * Global economic shifts and power dynamics. Very relevant to today.
* [Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System - Satoshi Nakamoto](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bitcoin:+A+Peer-to-Peer+Electronic+Cash+System+Satoshi+Nakamoto+buy)

* [The Wealth of Nations - Adam Smith](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Wealth+of+Nations+Adam+Smith+buy)
  * Foundational principles of economics.
