Intent as foundation

2026-01-15

If consciousness is fundamental, then intent is the foundation of any work.

Intent is shaped by the frequency through which you receive the stream of consciousness.

That frequency functions as a filter formed by your unique insights.

If the goal is software that reduces complexity down to pure intent, then build software that turns those unique insights into products.
